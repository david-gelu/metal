About Us

We are a team of engineers specialized in structural design, focused on the development of optimal and sustainable systems. The Metal Vision Design team has the capacity to design innovative and avant-garde engineering works. Our office is in Timisoara, Romania. In 2016 I have founded Metal Vision Design with the desire to make the structural design different, but better. From the beginning we chose as a destination a niche sector, 'Facade Engineering'.

MEng. Vlad D. Moș
Founder.

We design steel structures for the residential sector and the industrial sector.
We also offer façade engineering services, from curtain walling to custom envelopes.
